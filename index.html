FEATURES

Safety & Productivity,
unified.

KAVACH prioritizes safety above all, then makes workers faster and smarter — all hands-free.

🛡️

Real-Time Hazard Detection

Edge AI detects fall risks, machinery blind spots, and PPE violations in under 25ms — warns via instant audio alerts.

🧠

Zero-Hallucination Alerts

Cloud VLM is grounded by a SPARQL Knowledge Graph. Every alert cites an actual OSHA or IS regulation code — never invented.

📐

Hands-Free Blueprints

Workers ask questions by voice — the VLM queries the BIM model and speaks specs, torque values, and measurements aloud.

🔍

Tool & Inventory Tracking

Edge memory logs tool positions and material counts. Ask "Where's my drill?" and get an instant spoken answer.

📋

Automated Daily Logs

Full-day work tracking generates end-of-shift reports automatically. No more 45 minutes of unpaid admin work.

⚡

Offline Fallback

No Wi-Fi? Edge AI keeps running geometric heuristic safety checks and pre-recorded warnings. Safety never goes offline.
